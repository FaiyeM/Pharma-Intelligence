# 💊 Pharma Intelligence (CRM & CLM)

> A modern, executive-grade Life Sciences CRM and Closed Loop Marketing (CLM) environment. Styled to match leading enterprise platforms (like Veeva), it combines traditional healthcare provider rosters with real-time digital detailing slides, compliance sample tracking, roundtable events, and **Veeva-AI**—a server-side Gemini-powered coaching co-pilot.

## 📸 Screenshots Capture Guide

Follow these guidelines to take crisp, high-fidelity screenshots for your repository:

### 🛠️ Step 1: Prepare the Environment
1. Open the application in a **separate browser window** (rather than the embedded workspace iframe) to prevent UI truncation.
2. Toggle your browser to full screen (`F11` on Windows, `Cmd + Ctrl + F` on Mac).
3. Zoom out slightly to **90%** (via `Cmd/Ctrl + -`) to allow all widgets and graphs to breathe with optimal negative space.

### 📐 Step 2: Capture States
*   **Screenshot 1: Territory CRM Hub**
    *   *Path:* Dashboard Tab (`#pharma-app-root`)
    *   *Highlights:* Ensure the colorful roundtable RSVP counts and the starter pack inventory stock bars are visible.
*   **Screenshot 2: Interactive Detailing Tablet**
    *   *Path:* Digital eDetailing Tab
    *   *Action:* Toggle the dosing switch and write a signature in the box. Capture the entire layout including the custom line charts.
*   **Screenshot 3: Veeva-AI Pitch Gym Dialogue**
    *   *Path:* Pharma Co-pilot Tab
    *   *Action:* Run a quick pre-visit briefing query. Capture the generated markdown highlighting therapeutic terms and compliance benchmarks.

## 🚀 Key Technological Modules

1. **Cross-Module State Synchronization**: Submitting a sample drop during an eDetailing presentation triggers real-time state changes—updating the doctor's visit counters, recording call logs, and deducting physical quantities from the live sample stock.
2. **Server-Side Clinical Intelligence**: Connects to server-side Gemini models (`process.env.GEMINI_API_KEY`) using the robust `@google/genai` SDK to evaluate clinical pitch accuracy and provide compliant life-sciences sales coaching.
3. **Interactive Trial Simulation**: Interactive data visualization controls allow representatives to toggle dose structures and view therapeutic trial endpoint correlations dynamically.
